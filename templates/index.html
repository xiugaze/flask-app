About Oysters

Oysters are a type of mollusk known for their delicious taste and nutritional benefits. They can be enjoyed raw, steamed, grilled, or fried, and are a staple in many coastal cuisines around the world.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in essential minerals like zinc, iron, and calcium.
  • High in protein and low in fat.
  • Contains omega-3 fatty acids which are good for heart health.

Popular Varieties

There are many types of oysters, each with its unique flavor profile. Some of the most popular varieties include:

  • Blue Point
  • Kumamoto
  • Olympia
  • Belon
